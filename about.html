About Me

I graduated from the University of Liverpool in 2010 with a Masters Degree in Aerospace Engineering. I have worked for over a decade in a wide variety of roles within the aerospace industry, including avionic software development, structural analysis, advanced manufacturing, and systems engineering research.


In parallel to my aerospace career, I became a freelance software developer in 2012 and have worked on a diverse portfolio of projects ever since, My work has included Blockchain and Ethereum ERC20 development, php web development for eCommerce in Laravel, MetaTrader4 Expert Advisor programming, optimisation of structures using genetic evolutionary algorithms and machine controllers for large volume 3D printers.
